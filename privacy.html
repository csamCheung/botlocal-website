私隱政策 · Privacy Policy

最後更新 Last updated: 2026-07-07

1. 我哋係邊個 · Who we are

BotLocal 為香港小店及中小企提供 AI 客服機械人服務（WhatsApp、網站 chat widget、Facebook Messenger 及 Instagram DM）。

BotLocal provides AI customer-service chatbot services (WhatsApp, website chat widget, Facebook Messenger and Instagram DM) for small businesses in Hong Kong.

2. 我哋收集嘅資料 · Data we collect

當你透過 WhatsApp 或其他渠道同我哋嘅機械人對話，我哋會處理：你嘅訊息內容、你嘅電話號碼或帳號識別碼，以及你自願留低嘅聯絡資料（例如姓名、電郵）。

When you chat with our bots via WhatsApp or other channels, we process: your message content, your phone number or account identifier, and any contact details you voluntarily provide (e.g. name, email).

3. 資料點樣使用 · How data is used

資料只用嚟回答你嘅查詢、跟進你留低嘅聯絡要求，以及改善機械人嘅回答質素。我哋唔會將你嘅資料賣畀無關第三方，亦唔會同無關第三方分享，更唔會用嚟訓練其他公司嘅機械人。

Data is used only to answer your enquiries, follow up on contact requests you leave, and improve answer quality. We never sell or share your data with unrelated third parties, and never use it to train other companies' bots.

4. 第三方服務 · Third-party services

訊息透過 Meta 嘅 WhatsApp Business Platform 傳送；AI 回覆由 OpenAI 等大型語言模型服務產生；系統寄存喺雲端伺服器（Railway，歐洲區）。呢啲服務商各自有其私隱政策。

Messages are delivered via Meta's WhatsApp Business Platform; AI replies are generated by large-language-model providers such as OpenAI; the system is hosted on cloud servers (Railway, EU region). Each provider has its own privacy policy.

5. 資料保留與刪除 · Retention & deletion

你可以隨時要求我哋刪除你嘅對話紀錄同聯絡資料。請電郵 hello@botlocal.hk 或 WhatsApp +44 7472 323783。

You may request deletion of your conversation history and contact details at any time via hello@botlocal.hk or WhatsApp +44 7472 323783.

6. 政策更新 · Changes

如本政策有重大更新，我哋會喺本頁公佈。繼續使用服務即表示你同意最新版本。

Material changes will be posted on this page. Continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of the latest version.
