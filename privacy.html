私隱政策 · Privacy Policy

最後更新 Last updated: 2026-07-18

1. 我哋係邊個 · Who we are

BotLocal（以下簡稱「我哋」）以「BotLocal」為商業名稱，為小店及中小企提供 AI 客服助理 SaaS 服務。現時可用嘅渠道為 WhatsApp 同網站即時對話（website live chat）；Instagram、Facebook Messenger、Email 同 SMS 即將推出。

BotLocal ("we", "us") operates under the trading name "BotLocal" and provides an AI customer-service assistant SaaS for small businesses. Channels available today are WhatsApp and website live chat; Instagram, Facebook Messenger, Email and SMS are coming soon.

2. 我哋喺兩種角色下處理資料 · Two roles in which we handle data

(a) 作為資料控制者：我哋處理你（商業客戶）用嚟開設同管理帳戶嘅資料，例如你嘅姓名、公司名、電郵、WhatsApp 號碼同帳單資料。
(b) 作為資料處理者：當你嘅客人同你嘅 bot 對話時，我哋代你（商業客戶）處理呢啲對話資料。呢啲資料屬於你，我哋只會按你嘅指示、為咗提供服務而處理。

(a) As a data controller for the account data you (our business customer) use to sign up and manage your account — e.g. your name, business name, email, WhatsApp number and billing details.
(b) As a data processor for the end-customer conversation data we process on your behalf when your customers chat with your bot. That data belongs to you; we process it only on your instructions and to provide the service.

3. 我哋收集／處理嘅資料 · Data we collect / process

當客人透過 WhatsApp 或網站即時對話同 bot 對話，我哋會處理：訊息內容、電話號碼或帳號識別碼，以及客人自願留低嘅聯絡資料（例如姓名、電郵）。商業客戶帳戶方面，我哋會處理登入、聯絡同帳單所需嘅資料。

When a customer chats with a bot via WhatsApp or website live chat, we process: message content, phone number or account identifier, and any contact details the customer voluntarily provides (e.g. name, email). For business accounts, we process the data needed for login, contact and billing.

4. 資料點樣使用 · How data is used

資料只用嚟：回答查詢、跟進客人留低嘅聯絡要求、營運同改善服務、處理帳單。我哋唔會將資料賣畀無關第三方，亦唔會用你嘅資料訓練其他公司嘅模型。

Data is used only to: answer enquiries, follow up on contact requests, operate and improve the service, and handle billing. We never sell data to unrelated third parties, and we never use your data to train other companies' models.

5. 第三方服務 · Third-party services

為咗提供服務，我哋會使用可靠嘅供應商：訊息透過 Meta 嘅 WhatsApp Business Platform 傳送；AI 回覆由大型語言模型服務（例如 OpenAI）產生；系統寄存喺雲端伺服器（Railway，歐洲區）；訂閱付款（上線後）由 Paddle 處理，佢作為 Merchant of Record 收款。每個供應商各自有其私隱政策。

To provide the service we use trusted providers: messages are delivered via Meta's WhatsApp Business Platform; AI replies are generated by large-language-model providers such as OpenAI; the system is hosted on cloud servers (Railway, EU region); and subscription payments (once live) are handled by Paddle as Merchant of Record. Each provider has its own privacy policy.

6. 資料保留與刪除 · Retention & deletion

對話同帳戶資料只會喺提供服務所需嘅期間保留。你可以隨時要求我哋刪除你嘅對話紀錄同聯絡資料；商業客戶終止服務後，我哋會喺合理時間內刪除或匿名化相關資料（法律要求保留嘅除外）。請透過下方聯絡方法提出。

Conversation and account data is kept only for as long as needed to provide the service. You may request deletion of your conversation history and contact details at any time; after a business customer ends the service, we delete or anonymise the related data within a reasonable period (except where the law requires retention). Please use the contact details below.

7. 你嘅權利 · Your rights

視乎你所在地區，你可能享有查閱、更正、刪除、限制或反對處理你個人資料嘅權利，包括英國 GDPR（UK GDPR）及香港《個人資料（私隱）條例》（PDPO）下嘅權利。要行使呢啲權利，請聯絡我哋。

Depending on where you are, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict or object to the processing of your personal data, including rights under the UK GDPR and Hong Kong's Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance (PDPO). To exercise these rights, contact us.

Material changes will be posted on this page. Continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of the latest version.
