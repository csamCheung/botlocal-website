退款政策 · Refund Policy

最後更新 Last updated: 2026-07-18

1. 先試後買 · Try before you pay

Basic 同 Professional 方案有 14 天免費試用，免信用卡。你可以喺試用期內實際用個 AI 客服，覺得啱先繼續。試用期完結前，你唔會被收取任何費用。

The Basic and Professional plans include a 14-day free trial, no card required. You can actually use the AI assistant during the trial and only continue if it's right for you. You are not charged anything before the trial ends.

2. 月費 · Monthly fees

當一個計費週期開始，該期嘅月費一般不予退還，因為服務喺整個週期內都持續提供。以下情況除外（見第 3 節）。

Once a billing period starts, that period's monthly fee is generally non-refundable, because the service is provided throughout the period. Exceptions apply (see section 3).

3. 我哋會退款嘅情況 · When we do refund

我哋會就以下情況退款：
· 重複收費——同一期被重複扣款；
· 帳單錯誤——因我哋或付款系統出錯而多收；
· 法定權利——適用消費者法例賦予你嘅退款權利，我哋一律尊重。

We will issue a refund for:
· Duplicate charges — being billed twice for the same period;
· Billing errors — overcharges caused by us or the payment system;
· Statutory rights — any refund rights you have under applicable consumer law, which we always honour.

4. 點樣申請 · How to request

請透過電郵 samkiengineer@gmail.com 或 WhatsApp +44 7472 323783 聯絡我哋，並附上你嘅帳戶資料同相關收費詳情。我哋會喺 5 個工作天內回覆。獲批嘅退款會退返去原本嘅付款方式。

Contact us by email at samkiengineer@gmail.com or WhatsApp +44 7472 323783 with your account details and the charge in question. We respond within 5 business days. Approved refunds go back to the original payment method.

5. 取消 · Cancellation

你可以隨時取消，取消會停止將來嘅收費——當期已開始嘅月費不予退還，之後就唔會再收費。取消唔會即時中斷服務，你可以用到當期完結為止。

You can cancel anytime; cancellation stops future charges — the fee for a period already started is non-refundable, and you won't be billed after that. Cancelling does not cut off service immediately; you keep access until the end of the current period.

6. 付款處理 · Payment processing

訂閱付款（上線後）由 Paddle 作為 Merchant of Record 處理。你嘅收據同帳單可能會顯示 Paddle 嘅名稱。

Subscription payments (once live) are handled by Paddle as Merchant of Record. Your receipt and statement may show Paddle's name.
